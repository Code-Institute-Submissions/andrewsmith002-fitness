About


I started training in the gym and martial arts when I was 15 years old and 11 years old respectively. The first martial arts I trained in was Kempo Karate for 3 years. After that i decided to try another sytle of martial arts which was YMAA Kung-Fu. I decided to practice this and others because I am a big fan of Bruce Lee, Chuck Norris, Jean-Calude Van Damme to name a few. I practiced this style for 5 years and then I changed to Taekwondo. I now practice both styles of Taekwondo, ITF and WTF.

I am a qualified personal trainer and I work in Energie Fitness. I specialise in Strength and Conditioning, Bodybuilding, Flexibility and fitness for Martial Arts. I am Spin qualified and teach that class a few times a few times a week. I also teach group exercise classes such as circuit classes, strength classes and ab classes.

Online Training Plans

Strength and Conditioning


Strength and Conditioning focuses on ways to improve athletic performance including endurance, speed, strength and power. A program will be designed specifically for each individual that aims to address the individual's weakness and to develop their strengths

Martial Arts Fitness and Flexibility


Martial arts fitness and flexibility program involves gettig fit by using punching and kicking techniques with other fitness-based conditioning exercises. Each program will be include stretching exercises pre-workout and post-workout designed specifially for martial artists.

Bodybuilding


This personalised workout program will focus specifically on muscle hypertrophy which is an increase and growth of muscle cells through exercise using weights. Each program will be for 12 weeks.
